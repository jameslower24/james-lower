About Me

I'm a Pre-Business student at BYU with recent experience in real estate underwriting, alternative investments, and financial modeling. I am currently working on a live private equity deal.

Before coming to BYU I lived in Ecuador for two years as a volunteer representative for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Upon returning, I have continued to lead international service trips and hope to minor in Global and Community Impact, eventually starting my own nonprofit to help educate and employ people in developing countries.

Outside of school and finance I enjoy spending time with my fiancé, running up Provo Canyon, and traveling internationally.
